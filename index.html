About me

I'm Ada and

I am an Apple Developer Academy graduate student, born and raised in Naples. I consider myself a strong and passionate individual with particular care for details, who can work under pressure and with strict deadlines. I only have one rule: listen who's around you. As a designer, I work to make the best possible User Experience and Interface. But for doing that, you must study and understand your user and his needs. Then, you can start creating things. 

I've always considered the communication extremely important, and working with different people and approaching different fields, helped me to understand the value of listening to other voices -even the ones that we tend to ignore, or that asks us more attention to be heard. The minorities, the different ones; being able to make apps that can help who is actually in need, is probably one of the things that satisfy me the most.
